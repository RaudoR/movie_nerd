Welcome to MovieNerd!

This is a simple movie information site, No ads, No confusion. Just straight to the point, find information about your favorite movies.

This site uses the OMDb API, a public movie database inspired on IMDB. It still needs some polish but overall it is a nice resource. Enjoy the app :).
